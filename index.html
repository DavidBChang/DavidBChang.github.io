David Chang

I am a high school senior at Northwood High School in Irvine, California. I have been a competitor in Team America Rocketry Challenge, the world's largest student model rocketry competition for about three years and Nasa's Student Launch for about 1 year. I am the electronics lead of the TARC team and the payload lead for Student Launch. My team's mentor is Bob Koepke, who is a director at the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics, AIAA for short.
